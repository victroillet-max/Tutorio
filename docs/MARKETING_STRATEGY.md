# Tutorio Marketing Strategy
## Comprehensive Go-To-Market Plan for Swiss EdTech Platform

**Version**: 1.0  
**Date**: January 2025  
**Status**: Strategic Planning

---

## Executive Summary

Tutorio is positioned to disrupt the overpriced private tutoring market in Switzerland by offering AI-structured educational content at a fraction of the cost. This marketing strategy leverages product-led growth (PLG) combined with targeted acquisition to capture the Swiss university student market, with plans for European expansion.

**Key Value Proposition**: "Stop Overpaying for Tutoring" - Quality business school content at CHF 10-25/month vs. CHF 80+/hour for private tutors.

---

## 2. ICP (Ideal Customer Profile) Definition

### 2.1 Primary Target Audience

**Demographics**:
- **Age**: 18-26 years old
- **Education**: Bachelor's/Master's students at Swiss business schools
- **Location**: French-speaking Switzerland (primary), German-speaking Switzerland (secondary)
- **Universities**: HEC Lausanne, EPFL, Uni Geneva, Uni St. Gallen, ETH Zurich
- **Budget**: Limited student budget, price-sensitive

**Psychographics**:
- **Pain Level**: 8-9/10 (struggling with course material, can't afford tutoring)
- **Motivation**: Pass exams, improve grades, understand complex concepts
- **Study Habits**: Mix of self-study, group study, and some paid resources
- **Tech Savvy**: High (digital natives, comfortable with online learning)
- **Risk Tolerance**: Willing to try new platforms if free trial available

**Behavioral Characteristics**:
- Searches for course summaries, study guides, and exam prep materials
- Active on student forums, WhatsApp groups, and university social channels
- Tends to cram before exams (seasonal demand spikes)
- Word-of-mouth influenced by peers
- Values convenience and mobile accessibility

## 3. Positioning & Messaging

### 3.1 Positioning Framework (April Dunford Method)

**Step 1: Competitive Alternatives**

What would customers use if Tutorio didn't exist?

| Alternative | Type | Pros | Cons |
|-------------|------|------|------|
| Private tutors | Direct | Personalized, exam-focused | CHF 60-120/hour, expensive |
| YouTube tutorials | Indirect | Free, accessible | Generic, not Swiss curriculum |
| Course slides/notes | Status quo | Free, from professors | Confusing, not structured |
| Study groups | Status quo | Free, social | Time-consuming, inconsistent |
| Do nothing | Status quo | No effort | Fail exams |
| Chegg/Course Hero | Direct | Large content library | Expensive, not Swiss-focused |

**Step 2: Unique Attributes**

What does Tutorio have that alternatives don't?

1. **Swiss Curriculum Alignment** - Content designed specifically for HEC, EPFL, Uni Geneva courses
2. **AI-Structured Summaries** - Complex topics condensed into digestible, exam-focused content
3. **Interactive Exercises** - Practice problems with step-by-step solutions
4. **Progress Tracking** - Visual dashboard showing mastery and study streaks
5. **Affordable Pricing** - CHF 10-25/month vs. CHF 80+/hour for tutors
6. **Created by Students** - Content created by students who excelled in these courses

**Step 3: Value Mapping**

| Attribute | Value | Outcome |
|-----------|-------|---------|
| Swiss curriculum alignment | Relevant, exam-focused content | Higher exam scores |
| AI-structured summaries | Faster comprehension | Study in half the time |
| Interactive exercises | Active learning, not passive | Better retention |
| Affordable pricing | 90% cost savings | Access tutoring-quality content on student budget |
| Progress tracking | Motivation and accountability | Consistent study habits |

**Step 4: Best-Fit Customers**

Swiss business school students (Years 1-2) who:
- Are struggling with or want to excel in core courses (Accounting, Computational Thinking)
- Cannot afford private tutoring
- Prefer self-paced, online learning
- Need exam-focused content specifically for their university

**Step 5: Market Category**

**Chosen Position**: "Big Fish, Small Pond"
- Own the niche: **Affordable online tutoring for Swiss business students**
- Not competing with Coursera/Udemy (generic courses)
- Not competing with YouTube (not structured, not Swiss-focused)
- Differentiated from tutors (price) and free resources (quality)

**Step 6: Relevant Trends**

- **Rising tuition costs** → Students seeking affordable alternatives
- **AI in education** → Students expect intelligent, personalized learning
- **Post-COVID online learning acceptance** → Comfort with digital education
- **Subscription economy** → Students used to Netflix/Spotify model
- **Mobile-first generation** → Expect learning on-the-go

### 4.2 Competitive Battlecard: Private Tutors

```
COMPETITOR: Private Tutors (Local tutoring market)

OVERVIEW:
- Market: CHF 100M+ annually in Switzerland
- Pricing: CHF 60-120/hour
- Model: 1:1, in-person or online sessions
- Availability: Limited, often booked

POSITIONING:
- They say: "Personalized attention for better grades"
- Reality: Expensive, inconsistent quality, availability issues

KEY STRENGTHS:
1. Personalized feedback (instant Q&A)
2. Adaptable to student needs
3. Accountability partner

KEY WEAKNESSES:
1. Expensive (CHF 80+ per session)
2. Scheduling conflicts
3. Variable quality (no standardization)
4. Not available 24/7

TUTORIO ADVANTAGES:
1. 90% cost savings (CHF 25 vs. CHF 200/month for 2-3 sessions)
2. Available 24/7, study when it suits you
3. Consistent quality (expert-reviewed content)
4. AI-structured for efficient learning

WHEN WE WIN:
- Price-sensitive students
- Self-motivated learners
- Busy schedules (can't commit to tutor times)
- Prefer self-paced learning

WHEN WE LOSE:
- Need instant 1:1 feedback
- Struggling with specific homework problems
- Prefer human interaction
- Parents are paying (less price-sensitive)

TALK TRACKS:

Objection: "I need personalized help from a real person"
Response: "Totally understand - everyone learns differently. That's why we also have an AI tutor that can answer your questions 24/7. Why not try our free preview? Many students find they get 80% of the value at 10% of the cost."

Objection: "Tutors can explain things my way"
Response: "Absolutely, and that personalized touch is valuable. What we offer is AI-structured content that breaks down complex topics step-by-step, with multiple explanation approaches. Plus, our interactive exercises give you immediate feedback. You might find it covers most of what you need."

PROOF POINTS:
- "My grade went from a C to an A-!" - Marie S., HEC Lausanne
- "Way cheaper than hiring a tutor, and honestly more effective" - Lucas B., EPFL
- Average rating: 4.9/5 from 50+ students
```

## 5. Go-To-Market (GTM) Strategy

### 5.1 GTM Motion: Product-Led Growth (PLG) + Targeted Acquisition

**Chosen Model**: Hybrid PLG + Community Marketing

**Why PLG**:
- Students are price-sensitive (free trial reduces friction)
- Product demonstrates value quickly (instant access to content)
- Viral potential (students share with peers)
- Low touch (scalable without large sales team)

**Entry Funnel**:
```
AWARENESS
↓ (Content marketing, social, word-of-mouth)
FREE PREVIEW
↓ (5+ lessons per course available free)
ACCOUNT CREATION
↓ (Email signup, no credit card)
ACTIVE USAGE
↓ (Complete exercises, track progress)
PAID CONVERSION
↓ (Hit paywall on advanced content)
SUBSCRIPTION (Basic CHF 10 / Premium CHF 25)
↓
EXPANSION (Upgrade to Premium, add courses)
↓
ADVOCACY (Referrals, testimonials)
```

### 5.2 Channel Strategy

**Primary Channels** (invest heavily):

| Channel | Why | Tactics | Budget |
|---------|-----|---------|--------|
| University Partnerships | Direct access to students | Bulletin boards, newsletters, student portals | CHF 5k/year |
| Student Ambassadors | Peer trust, word-of-mouth | Recruit 10-20 ambassadors, incentivize referrals | CHF 10k/year |
| Instagram/TikTok | Where students are | Educational content, memes, study tips | CHF 15k/year |
| SEO Content | Long-term traffic | "Financial Accounting HEC summary" type keywords | CHF 5k/year |

**Secondary Channels** (test and learn):

| Channel | Why | Tactics | Budget |
|---------|-----|---------|--------|
| LinkedIn Ads | Reach business students | Targeted ads to students at Swiss universities | CHF 5k/year |
| Reddit/Student Forums | Community presence | Answer questions, share helpful content | Free |
| WhatsApp Groups | Peer communities | Partner with student group admins | Free |
| Email Marketing | Nurture signups | Exam prep sequences, study tips | CHF 2k/year |

**Avoid These Channels** (for now):
- Google Search Ads (expensive for student market)

| Period | Activity Level | Marketing Focus |

| **September** | High (semester start) | Acquisition push, "start strong" messaging |
| October | Medium | Content marketing, engagement |
| **November-December** | Peak (midterms) | Conversion push, "ace your midterms" |
| January | Low (holidays/exams over) | Retention, community building |

## 6. Student Ambassador Program

### 6.1 Program Overview

**Objective**: Create a network of student ambassadors who promote Tutorio within their universities in exchange for incentives.

**Why This Works**:
- Students trust peer recommendations
- Low-cost acquisition channel
- Authentic advocacy (they use the product)
- Campus presence without expensive field marketing

### 6.2 Ambassador Structure

**Recruitment Criteria**:
- Currently enrolled at target university
- Active on social media (Instagram/TikTok preferred)
- Good academic standing
- Enthusiastic about Tutorio after using it

**Ambassador Benefits**:
- Free Premium subscription (CHF 25/month value)
- Commission on referrals (CHF 5 per paid conversion)
- Exclusive merchandise
- LinkedIn recommendation/reference letter
- Community of like-minded students

**Ambassador Responsibilities**:
- Post 2x/month on social media about Tutorio
- Share referral link with classmates
- Attend monthly ambassador call (30 min)
- Provide feedback on product and content
- Represent Tutorio at university events (optional)

### 6.3 Referral Program

**For Ambassadors (Power Users)**:
- CHF 5 cash/credit per paid conversion
- Bonus: CHF 50 for every 10 referrals

**For Regular Users**:
- Give: Friend gets 1 week Premium free
- Get: 1 month added to subscription for each referral

**Tracking**:
- Unique referral codes per user
- Dashboard showing referral conversions
- Monthly payout or subscription credit

## 8. Pricing Strategy & Conversion

### 8.1 Current Pricing Model

| Plan | Price | Features |
|------|-------|----------|
| **Free Preview** | CHF 0 | 5+ lessons per course, limited exercises |
| **Basic** | CHF 10/month | AI summaries, all exercises, progress tracking |
| **Premium** | CHF 25/month | Everything in Basic + HD video, audio, exam simulations |

**Annual Pricing** (optional future):
- Basic Annual: CHF 99/year (save CHF 21)
- Premium Annual: CHF 249/year (save CHF 51)

### 8.2 Conversion Tactics

**Free → Paid Triggers**:
- Soft paywall after completing 5 free lessons
- "Unlock all exercises" prompt after first exercise completion
- Exam season messaging: "Premium users pass at higher rates"
- Time-limited offers: "50% off first month" during September

**Basic → Premium Upgrades**:
- In-app prompts when accessing video/audio content
- "Most Popular" badge on Premium in pricing page
- Testimonials from Premium users highlighting video value
- Limited-time Premium trials (3 days free)

### 8.3 Pricing Psychology

- **Anchor against tutoring**: "For the price of 15 minutes with a tutor, get a full month of Tutorio"
- **Reference pricing**: Show CHF 80+/hour tutor rates on pricing page
- **Social proof**: "50+ students already learning"
- **Low-risk entry**: "Start free, no credit card required"
- **Money-back guarantee**: "7-day refund if not satisfied"
